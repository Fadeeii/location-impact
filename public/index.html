International scale for categorizing asteroid impact hazards

Level 0

No Hazard (White)

Likelihood of collision is zero, or well below the chance that a random object will strike Earth

Level 1-4

Normal (Green)

Routine discovery with pass near Earth. Actual collision extremely unlikely. Current surveys should discover many such objects.

Level 5-7

Threatening (Yellow/Orange)

Close encounter with significant threat of regional to global catastrophe. Attention by astronomers required to provide precise forecast.

Level 8-10

Certain Collision (Red)

Collision is certain, capable of causing localized destruction (8), regional devastation (9), or global climate catastrophe (10).
